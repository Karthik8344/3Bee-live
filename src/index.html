About 3Bee

3Bee is an IoT system that, when applied to a beehive, collects biometric information about the colony and analyzes the quality of the environment. The device collects all relevant data about the colony (weight, temperature, humidity, sound intensity), which is then analyzed using artificial intelligence to determine the health of the hive and its environment. Data from the device can be integrated with laboratory sampling and analysis to determine the presence of pesticides or heavy metals.
